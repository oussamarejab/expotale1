Engineering used in transportation is one of the main branches of engineering. It integrates with other sectors and gains special importance due to its overlap with different specialization and the broad sectors that serve it. Transport Engineering is the primary application of scientific principles in planning, designing, operating, and Managing any of the land, air and sea transportation facilities in a safe, fast, comfortable, easy, economical and environmentally friendly way to transport people and goods..

Transport Engineering mainly concerned with studying and designing the facilities and infrastructure of the transport and transportation sector (land, sea and air transport facilities). In addition to conducting studies related to organizing and managing traffic movement within cities and on highways outside them. Those working in this field possess engineering skills that contribute to the implementation of economic development plans in the country and abroad, by promoting transport, and infrastructure projects in many Arab countries .
