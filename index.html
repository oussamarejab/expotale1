    Who We Are?

    Expo Tale Events and Production

    is a multi service company based in Qatar. We create, design and deliver events of all shapes and sizes throughout the country. with true events expertise on-board, Expo Tale can take care of event managementto make your job easier and deliver a spectacular result.

    Our Values

    Our core values are that we aim to ensure all of our events or services leave a lasting impression for all the right reasons. Our approach is to listen to your requirements and to deliver your event on-brief, within budget and to a standard that will exceed your expectations.

    Mission and Vision

    Our mission as an multi service company is to provide bespoke events and services to suit your strategic vision. We create and deliver events of all shapes and sizes that are designed to engage your audience through live experiences.
